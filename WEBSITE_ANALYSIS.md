## 5. WHAT IS SPEED DATING PAGE (WhatIsSpeedDating.aspx)

### Title
"What is Speed Dating"

### Content Sections

**1. What is speed dating?**
- Long explanation of the problem: wasting time on wrong dates
- Explanation of the 7-minute concept
- Why spend whole evening when you know in 5 minutes?

**2. How does it work?**
- Around 12 professional Jewish singles in age group
- Held at trendy bars
- Women stay seated, men rotate
- 7 minutes per date
- Bell rings to signal rotation
- Score card system
- Choice: "Date" or "Friend"
- Matching system explained
- Contact details revealed on mutual match

**3. During the evening**
- Check-in process
- Score card received
- Mingling time
- Table assignments
- Marking choices (Date/Friend)
- Breaks included
- Post-event mingling

**4. Login process**
- Username and password provided at ticket purchase
- Kept confidential

**5. Submitting your choices**
- Login to website
- Select event date
- View list of people met
- Choose Date/Friend for each
- Select contact details to reveal (email/phone)
- Match types explained

**6. Updating your details**
- Profile page access
- Change password option

**7. Forgotten password**
- Reset password link
- Email recovery system

**8. How do I get my match results?**
- Login to view matches
- Submit choices promptly
- Check back multiple times
- Email reminder after 3 days
- Results email sent after 1 week

**9. "No match yet" explained**
- Means person hasn't entered choices

**10. Offline options**
- Hand score card to hostess
- Email choices to info@TheSpeedDating.co.uk
- Call 0700 3401347

**11. Why do so many people come to speed dating?**
- Meet many singles in one evening vs. months separately

**12. What do we think this night is all about?**
- Finding dates AND friends
- Networking opportunity
- Practicing communication skills
- Relaxed atmosphere
- Spontaneity encouraged

**13. Some tips**
- Be who you are
- First impressions matter
- Balance talking and listening
- Avoid repetitive questions
- Be creative
- Make it entertaining
- Enjoy dating for what it is
- Most people have something interesting to offer

### Call to Action

- Encourages leaving feedback
- Success statistics: 120 couples married

## SUMMARY

TheSpeedDating.co.uk is a well-established Jewish speed dating service operating since 2003, with a proven track record of 120+ marriages. The website offers:

- **Core Service:** Speed dating events (7-minute dates, 10-12 people per evening)
- **Premium Service:** VIP membership with discounts and perks
- **Personalized Service:** Jewish Matches matchmaking (£700-1450)
- **Multi-platform:** Events in UK, US, France, Israel
- **Strong Social Proof:** Extensive testimonials and success stories
- **Professional Execution:** High-class venues, organized processes

The site features comprehensive event management, user registration, VIP memberships, and detailed information about how speed dating works. Design is dated but functional, with clear navigation and strong call-to-actions throughout.
